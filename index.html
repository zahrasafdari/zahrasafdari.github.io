About

I am a Master's student in Computer Engineering at Bilkent University (Sep 2025–Sep 2027), working as a Research & Teaching Assistant in the ASAP Lab under the supervision of Dr. Sinem Sav.

My current research at ASAP Lab focuses on privacy-preserving machine learning and genomic beacon defense — designing systems that protect individuals' sensitive information without sacrificing scientific utility. Prior to joining Bilkent, I worked remotely as a Research Assistant at Missouri University of Science and Technology under Dr. Suman Kalyan Maity (Dec 2024–Sep 2025).

I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Tabriz (Sep 2020–Sep 2024). I also did an internship at the IPM Institute for Research in Fundamental Sciences.

Research & Projects

🧬
Genomic Beacon Defense
Designing defenses against membership inference attacks on genomic Beacon systems to protect sensitive genetic information while preserving the utility of shared public health databases.
🌐
ParsCN — Persian Counter-Narrative Dataset
First comprehensive Persian counter-narrative dataset (1,100 pairs) built via a multi-stage framework combining expert annotation with LLM augmentation and semantic retrieval.
🤖
Augmented-Hater MSCBT
Multi-stacked CNN-BiLSTM-Transformer system for detecting hate speech across 7 languages, designed for integration into social media content moderation APIs.
🧊
Arctic Sea Ice Forecasting (EDACoL)
Hybrid ConvLSTM model with dual multi-head attention, fusing daily and monthly climate data to predict Arctic sea ice concentration.
🌍
SentiGerman
Led a team at IPM Institute applying BERT and RoBERTa to German sentiment classification — applicable in social media monitoring and customer feedback analysis.
⚖️
Fair Job Success Prediction
Interpretable hiring prediction system with bias mitigation and explainability to enable fair, ethical AI-driven recruitment decisions in HR technology.
