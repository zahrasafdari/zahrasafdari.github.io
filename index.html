About

I am a Master's student in Computer Engineering at Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey, where I also serve as a Research and Teaching Assistant at the ASAP Lab under the mentorship of Dr. Sinem Sav.

My research sits at the intersection of privacy-preserving machine learning (PPML), genomic data security, natural language processing, and social computing. I am particularly passionate about building AI systems that are robust, privacy-aware, and culturally inclusive—especially for underrepresented and low-resource languages such as Persian.

Before joining Bilkent, I completed my undergraduate studies in Computer Engineering at the University of Tabriz, Iran, where I developed a strong foundation in machine learning and software systems.

ParsCN: A Persian Dataset for Counter-Narrative Generation to Combat Online Hate Speech
Zahra Safdari Fesaghandis, Suman Kalyan Maity
arXiv:2603.27011 · Bilkent University & Missouri University of Science and Technology
We introduce ParsCN, the first and most comprehensive Persian counter-narrative dataset, consisting of 1,100 hate speech and counter-narrative pairs with fine-grained annotations across six target groups and six countering strategies. A novel multi-stage framework integrates culturally-informed human annotation with few-shot LLM-augmented generation, enabling high-quality, diverse counter-narratives while significantly reducing annotation costs.

Multilingual Hate Speech Detection and Counterspeech Generation: A Comprehensive Survey and Practical Guide
Zahra Safdari Fesaghandis, Suman Kalyan Maity
arXiv:2603.19279 · Bilkent University & Missouri University of Science and Technology
A comprehensive survey and practical guide to multilingual hate speech detection and counterspeech generation, integrating recent NLP advances. We analyze why monolingual systems fail in non-English and code-mixed contexts and outline a structured three-phase framework—task design, data curation, and evaluation—drawing on state-of-the-art datasets, models, and metrics to advance inclusive online safety.

Research & Projects

🛡️
Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning
Investigating techniques such as differential privacy, federated learning, and secure multi-party computation to enable machine learning on sensitive datasets without compromising individual privacy. Conducted at ASAP Lab under Dr. Sinem Sav.
🧬
Genomic Beacon Defense
Designing defenses against membership inference attacks on genomic data shared through Beacon systems. The goal is to protect individuals' sensitive genetic information while preserving the utility of public health databases.
🌐
ParsCN Dataset
Built the first comprehensive Persian counter-narrative dataset (1,100 pairs). Developed a scalable multi-stage annotation framework integrating human expertise and LLM augmentation with semantic retrieval, establishing a replicable paradigm for low-resource settings.
📊
Multilingual NLP & Social Computing
Studying how large language models can be adapted for multilingual hate speech detection and counter-narrative generation across culturally diverse contexts—emphasizing fairness, bias mitigation, and cross-lingual transferability.
🔍
LLM Evaluation & Benchmarking
Designing comprehensive evaluation frameworks for LLMs using automatic metrics (BERTScore, BLEU, ROUGE, METEOR, Toxicity) and human evaluation. Applied to assess mBART, PersianMind, GPT-4o, and Claude on Persian NLP tasks.
💬
Online Safety & Counter-Hate Systems
Building AI-powered systems that promote online safety through constructive counterspeech rather than content removal—balancing persuasiveness, empathy, and cultural sensitivity across diverse linguistic environments.
