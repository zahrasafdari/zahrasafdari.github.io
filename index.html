About Me

Hello! 👋 I’m Zahra Safdari, a dedicated computer scientist with a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Tabriz, where I ranked 4th in my class. My academic journey has fueled a deep passion for Applied Machine Learning, particularly within the fields of Climate Informatics and Social Computing—areas where I’m eager to continue innovating and making impactful contributions. 🌟

Under the guidance of esteemed mentors like Prof. Habib Izadkhah, I have immersed myself in cutting-edge research, focusing on Text Classification, Causal AI, and Trustworthy AI. One of my most significant achievements is co-authoring a research paper on a novel hybrid CNN-BiLSTM-Transformer model designed for multilingual hate speech detection. This project not only honed my technical skills but also solidified my commitment to advancing AI for social good.
