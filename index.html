About

I am a Master's student in Computer Engineering at Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey.

I work as a Research & Teaching Assistant at the ASAP Lab under Dr. Sinem Sav, focusing on privacy-preserving machine learning and genomic beacon defense. Prior to joining Bilkent, I was a Research Assistant at Missouri University of Science and Technology under Dr. Suman Kalyan Maity, where I worked on multilingual hate speech and counter-narrative generation.

I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science at the University of Tabriz. My undergraduate research spanned Social Computing, NLP and Ethical AI under Prof. Habib Izadkhah, with an internship at the IPM Institute on multilingual sentiment analysis.

Publications

Accepted · ICWSM 2026
ParsCN: A Persian Dataset for Counter-Narrative Generation to Combat Online Hate Speech
Zahra Safdari Fesaghandis, Suman Kalyan Maity
arXiv:2603.27011 · Bilkent University & Missouri University of Science and Technology
We introduce ParsCN, the first and most comprehensive Persian counter-narrative dataset — 1,100 hate speech and counter-narrative pairs with fine-grained annotations across six target groups and six countering strategies. A novel multi-stage framework integrates culturally-informed human annotation with few-shot LLM-augmented generation guided by semantic retrieval, enabling high-quality diverse counter-narratives while significantly reducing annotation costs.

Multilingual Hate Speech Detection and Counterspeech Generation: A Comprehensive Survey and Practical Guide
Zahra Safdari Fesaghandis, Suman Kalyan Maity
arXiv:2603.19279 · Bilkent University & Missouri University of Science and Technology
A comprehensive survey and practical guide to multilingual hate speech detection and counterspeech generation. We analyze why monolingual systems fail in non-English and code-mixed contexts and present a structured three-phase framework—task design, data curation, and evaluation—drawing on state-of-the-art datasets, models, and metrics to advance inclusive online safety.
