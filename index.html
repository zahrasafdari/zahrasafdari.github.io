About Me

Hello! 👋 I’m Zahra Safdari, a passionate computer scientist with a degree in Computer Science from the University of Tabriz. Throughout my academic journey, I developed a strong interest in Applied Machine Learning, particularly in the realms of Climate Informatics and Social Computing. These areas captivate me, and I am committed to continuous learning and innovation within them. 🌟

Under the mentorship of esteemed professors like Prof. Habib Izadkhah, I engaged in research focusing on Text Classification, Causal AI, and Trustworthy AI. One of my proudest accomplishments is co-authoring a research paper that introduces a hybrid CNN-BILSTM-Transformer model for multilingual hate speech detection.
